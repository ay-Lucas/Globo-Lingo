Vocabulary:
- Doctor = Médico
- Teacher = Maestro
- Engineer = Ingeniero
- Lawyer = Abogado
- Artist = Artista
- Pilot = Piloto
- Nurse = Enfermero

Phrases:
- What does a... do? = ¿Qué hace un...?
- I want to be a... = Quiero ser...
- Do you have experience as a...? = ¿Tienes experiencia como...?

Examples:
- Quiero ser piloto. (I want to be a pilot.)
- ¿Qué hace un chef? (What does a chef do?)
