Vocabulary:
- Mother = Madre
- Father = Padre
- Brother = Hermano
- Sister = Hermana
- Family = Familia
- Love = Amor
- Friend = Amigo

Phrases:
- Who is your...? = ¿Quién es tu...?
- I love my... = Amo a mi...
- Tell me about your family = Cuéntame sobre tu familia

Examples:
- Amo a mi madre. (I love my mother.)
- Este es mi hermano. (This is my brother.)
